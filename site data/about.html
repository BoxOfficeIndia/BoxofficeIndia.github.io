About Us

Box office Pan India ( boxofficeindia.github.io ) is a webiste which gives collection reports & advance saless reports of Indian movies. It started in 2022.

We report boxoffice data, Worldwide Gross, Distributor Share, India nett, overseas hourly gross report for PanIdia movies of various languages lik telugu , tamil , hindi , malayalam , kannada & Industries like Tollywood, Kollywood, Bollywood, Hollywood.

We also report advance sales gross & bookings of Indian movies via various online platforms like BookMyShow, Paytm Movies, Ticketnew. We also report overseas advance sales of Indian in countries like USA, UK, Australia, SriLanka etc.
